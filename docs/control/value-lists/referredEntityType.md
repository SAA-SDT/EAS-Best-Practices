# referredEntityType
**Scope Note:**
To indicate the type of entity referred to in a narrative text.

- EAD3 Tag Library: [https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html](https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html)

---

## corpName
**Scope Note:**
Identifies the name of an organization or group of people that act as an organizational entity. Examples include names of associations, institutions, business firms, nonprofit enterprises, governments, government agencies, projects, programs, religious bodies, churches, conferences, athletic contests, exhibitions, expeditions, fairs, and ships.

- [https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-corpname](https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-corpname)

## famName
**Scope Note:**
Identifies the name of a group of persons closely related by blood or persons who form a household, and are related to the materials being described. Includes single families and family groups, e.g., Patience Parker Family and Parker Family.

- [https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-famname](https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-famname)

## function
**Scope Note:**
Identifies activities and processes that generated the described materials. Such terms often provide useful access points to the materials, especially for corporate, government, or institutional records. Examples include collecting taxes and entertaining.

- [https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-function](https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-function)

## genreForm
**Scope Note:**
Identifies the types of material being described by naming the style or technique of their intellectual content (genre), order of information or object function (form), and physical characteristics. Examples include: account books, architectural drawings, portraits, short stories, sound recordings, and videotapes.

- [https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-genreform](https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-genreform)

## geogName
**Scope Note:**
Identifies the name of a place, natural feature, or political jurisdiction. Examples include: Appalachian Mountains; Baltimore, MD; Chinatown, San Francisco; and Kew Gardens, England.

- [https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-geogname](https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-geogname)

## name
**Scope Note:**
The proper noun or noun phrase designation for an entity that is difficult to tag more specifically.

- [https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-name](https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-name)

## occupation
**Scope Note:**
Identifies a standardized form of the occupation if not provided in the element itself.

- [https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-occupation](https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-occupation)

## persName
**Scope Note:**
Identifies a name, including any or all forenames, surnames, honorific titles, and added names, of a person who is related to the materials being described as either a source, creator, or subject.

- [https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-persname](https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-persname)

## subject
**Scope Note:**
Indicates a topic reflected in the described materials.

- [https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-subject](https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-subject)

## title
**Scope Note:**
The name of an intellectual work, such as a monograph, serial, or painting, listed in a finding aid.

- [https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-title](https://www.loc.gov/ead/EAD3taglib/EAD3-TL-eng.html#elem-title)
