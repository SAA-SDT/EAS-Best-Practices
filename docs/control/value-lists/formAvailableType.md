# formAvailableType
**Scope Note:**
To identify the type of instantiation, e.g. information content printed or manually inscribed on paper (one instantiation), subsequently scanned and saved as a PDF document (second instantiation), and later on OCR-ed or HTR-ed and saved in ALTO XML format (third instantiation).

- Records in Contexts Conceptual Model (RiC-CM), Version 1.0: [https://www.ica.org/app/uploads/2024/01/ric-cm-1.0_0.pdf](https://www.ica.org/app/uploads/2024/01/ric-cm-1.0_0.pdf)

---

## analogOriginal
**Scope Note:**
Analogue original instantiation like a charter, map, drawing, plan, paper file, paper folder, photo negatve, etc.

## analogDerived
**Scope Note:**
Analogue derived instantiation like micoforms, photo prints, paper copies, etc.

## digitalOriginal
**Scope Note:**
Digital original instantiation, i.e. born digital material like text documents, spreadsheets, emails, databases, image files etc.

## digitalDerived
**Scope Note:** 
Digital derived instantiations like digitised instantiations of analgue originals, but also derived digital instantiations from born-digital and digitised materials, e.g. a PDF of an email, a thumbnail or display version of a TIFF image file, the OCR-ed text of a digitised paper file etc.
