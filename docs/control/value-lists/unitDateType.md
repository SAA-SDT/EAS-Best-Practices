# unitDateType

---

## bulk
**Scope Note:**
The time period of the majority of the content in the materials being described

- SAA Dictionary, [https://dictionary.archivists.org/entry/bulk-dates.html](https://dictionary.archivists.org/entry/bulk-dates.html)

## inclusive
**Scope Note:**
The earliest and latest dates of the materials being described

- SAA Dictionary, [https://dictionary.archivists.org/entry/inclusive-dates.html](https://dictionary.archivists.org/entry/inclusive-dates.html)
